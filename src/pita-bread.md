# Pita bread

For this one I just googled around a bit and found a mix that works for me thats
very simple, easy and quick. :)

## Ingredients

- 100 g of flour
- 1/4 teaspoon of sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt
- 1/4 baking powder bag
- 60 g water

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Heat up non-stick pan
3. Bake both sides for a couple of minutes each
